Connected Account-

Event Tickets can only be issued by the organization and cannot be put on Blockchain by individuals

For Attendees - How

  • You need to visit the website of the organization hosting the event

  • Look for the Event section on the website and select the event you want to attend

  • Enter your personal details and make the payment to obtain the event ticket

  • Once you receive the ticket, you need to ensure that you keep it safe until the event date

  • On the day of the event, you need to present the ticket to gain entry to the venue

  • If the organization has issued Blockchain-based event tickets, you may need to connect your metamask wallet to access the ticket

  • Follow the instructions on the website to access and confirm your ticket on the Blockchain
